BIO

My research history has given me a wide knowledge of sample preparation (bacteria, plants, human biofluids, beverages) as well as functional proteomic, metabolomic, and lipidomic analyses by cutting edge mass spectrometry. I have analytical and maintenance experience on a wide array of mass spectrometers: MALDI-TOF (Bruker UltrafleXtreme, SCIEX 4800), GC-MS (Thermo PolarisQ), triple quadrupole mass spectrometers (SCIEX 6500) and high resolution mass spectrometers required for high-troughput proteomic and metabolomic studies (SCIEX 6600, Thermo Orbitrap Fusion and QE plus). My current research projects include: salivary proteomics and metabolomics; pro-resolving lipid mediators; uncultivable micro-organisms.
